Quick rules

How to play

  • Enter everybody's names and choose a themed secret pack.
  • Pass the device around. Most players see the same secret; the imposter does not.
  • The starting player is completely random—even the imposter can be chosen.
  • Starting with that player, each person gives one related clue.
  • With Hint word enabled, an imposter who starts gets a stronger show-specific clue.
  • Discuss who sounded suspicious, then vote as a group.
  • If every imposter is selected, the crew wins. Otherwise, the imposter wins.
